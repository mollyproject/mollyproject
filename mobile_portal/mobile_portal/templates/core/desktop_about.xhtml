m.ox.ac.uk
Mobile Oxford is a new service run for students and staff at the University of Oxford. Incorporating information and access to services from across the institution, Mobile Oxford is a central place to act as your guide in both the city and the University.

Features

  • Contact search

    Search the University contact directory with one-click access to e-mail or phone.

  • Maps

    Search for colleges, departments, restaurants, bus stops and much more. Bus stops have live arrival predictions, post boxes have collection times (where available), and University pages let you explore the relationships between them.

  • Podcasts

    Listen to any podcast published through the University's podcast service.

  • Webcams

    View webcams from across the city. Check the weather, the traffic, or simply wave at yourself.

  • News

    Read news from across the University to find out about events and recent developments.

  • Results releases

    Check whether your examination school has had its results released so you know when to check the Student Self-Service System.

  • Weather

    Check the latest weather reports from Oxford.
